## Employee Benefits and Policies

### Vacation Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to **15 days of paid vacation** annually. Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month.

**Key Points:**
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum carryover: 5 days to the next calendar year
- Unused vacation days beyond the carryover limit are forfeited
- Part-time employees receive prorated vacation days

### Remote Work Policy

TechCorp supports flexible work arrangements. Employees are allowed to work remotely **2 days per week** (typically Tuesday and Thursday).

**Requirements:**
- Must maintain reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM PT)
- Must attend all mandatory in-person meetings
- Remote work agreement must be signed annually

### Working Hours

**Standard Schedule:**
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Lunch break: 1 hour (typically 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
- Total: 40 hours per week

**Flexible Hours:**
Employees may adjust their schedule within the range of 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM, provided they:
- Complete 40 hours per week
- Are present during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM)
- Coordinate with their team

### Health Insurance

TechCorp provides comprehensive health insurance coverage through BlueCross BlueShield.

**Coverage Includes:**
- Medical, dental, and vision
- Mental health services
- Prescription drug coverage
- Annual health checkups

**Employee Contribution:**
- Individual: $50/month
- Family: $150/month

### Retirement Plan

**401(k) Plan:**
- Employer match: 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 4 years (25% per year)
- Eligible after 3 months of employment

### Professional Development

Annual budget: **$2,000 per employee** for:
- Conference attendance
- Online courses and certifications
- Professional books and materials
- Workshops and training programs

### Parental Leave

**Maternity Leave:** 16 weeks paid leave
**Paternity Leave:** 8 weeks paid leave
**Adoption Leave:** 12 weeks paid leave

All parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

## Code of Conduct

### Core Values

1. **Integrity**: We act with honesty and transparency
2. **Innovation**: We embrace creativity and new ideas
3. **Collaboration**: We work together to achieve common goals
4. **Excellence**: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do

## IT and Security Policies

### Equipment

All employees receive:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS)
- External monitor (27-inch)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headphones

### Data Security

**Required Practices:**
- Use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters)
- Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Report security incidents immediately

**Prohibited:**
- Sharing passwords
- Using personal devices for company data without approval
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate websites

### Software Licenses

All software must be properly licensed. Employees may request software through the IT portal at it.techcorp.com.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted **twice annually** (June and December).

**Review Process:**
1. Self-assessment submission
2. Manager evaluation
3. One-on-one review meeting
4. Goal setting for next period

**Rating Scale:**
- Exceeds Expectations
- Meets Expectations
- Needs Improvement
- Unsatisfactory

## Compensation

### Salary Reviews

Salaries are reviewed annually in January. Increases are based on:
- Performance review ratings
- Market benchmarks
- Company financial performance
- Individual contributions

### Bonus Structure

**Annual Performance Bonus:**
- Target: 10-20% of base salary
- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid in March following the review year
